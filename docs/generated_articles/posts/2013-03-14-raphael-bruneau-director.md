The 2013 Methane Expo took place March 12–15, 2013 in Vancouver, Canada. It was organized as a collaboration between the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the government of Canada. Conference proceedings are available [here](http://www.globalmethane.org/expo_canada13/proceedings.html) (Biothermica presentation below).
Mr. Raphael Bruneau, Director-Carbon Markets, provided an update on the company's ventilation air methane (VAM) destruction activities and the projects under development with Walter Energy in Alabama, USA.
