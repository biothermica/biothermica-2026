The 2012 US CMM Conference took place September 24, 2012 in Las Vegas, USA. It was organized by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. Conference proceedings are available [here](http://www.epa.gov/cmop/conf/cmm_conference_sept12.html) (Biothermica presentation below).
Mr. Raphael Bruneau, Director-Carbon Markets, provided an update on the company's ventilation air methane (VAM) destruction activities and the projects under development with Walter Energy in Alabama, USA.
