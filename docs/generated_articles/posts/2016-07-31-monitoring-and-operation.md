Biothermica Technologies renews its monitoring and operation mandate for the landfill gas extraction systems with the City of Québec for a period of at least 3 years.  This activity includes the Saint-Tite-des-Caps and the Beauport landfills.  Biothermica is pleased to continue the operation of the collection system and pumping and flaring stations.  In addition to the LFG flare at Saint-Tite-des-Caps, Biothermica now operate the LFG biological oxidation system at the Beauport landfill using a biofiltre developed by the CRIQ for the City of Québec.
